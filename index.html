Email setup

This app uses EmailJS to send reminders — free for up to 200 emails/month, no backend needed. Sign up at emailjs.com →

Reminders will be sent here
Account → General → Public Key in your EmailJS dashboard
Email Services section in your EmailJS dashboard
Email Templates section — use variables: {{to_email}} {{subject}} {{message}}
EmailJS template guide

In your EmailJS template editor, set:
• To Email: {{to_email}}
• Subject: {{subject}}
• Body: {{message}} (enable HTML content)

That's it — the app fills in all values automatically when sending reminders.
